About Me

Hello! I'm a cloud engineer specializing in designing, provisioning, and optimizing Azure and AWS workloads with Infrastructure-as-Code (Terraform plus automated CI/CD). I emphasize secure, cost-conscious architectures using modular Terraform, policy enforcement, and environment parity across dev, staging, and production.

I build scalable containerized and serverless solutions, integrate observability (logs, metrics, traces), and apply performance, governance, and cost optimization practices. I enjoy turning ideas into resilient, repeatable deployments through automation and clean, maintainable code.

My core competencies include Azure, AWS, Terraform, CI/CD, Kubernetes, and various modern cloud technologies.
